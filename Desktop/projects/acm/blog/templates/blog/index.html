About ACM

ACM brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges. Our primary goal here at the NYIT is to make a home for the computer science students where they can learn new standard to contribute and collaborate on projects.

For communication ACM uses a channel on Telegram messenger.
